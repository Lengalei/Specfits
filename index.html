Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of fashion and design services do you offer?

We specialize in custom clothing, accessories, and fashion consultancy. Whether you’re looking for tailored outfits, unique accessories, or help styling for a special event, our team is here to bring your vision to life.

2. Can I customize the curtains for my home or office?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable curtain-making services. You can choose from a wide range of fabrics, styles, and colors to match your space. Our team will also measure and install the curtains to ensure they fit perfectly.

3. Do you provide event decoration services for all types of events?

Yes! We cater to weddings, corporate events, parties, and more. Whether you're looking for elegant, modern, or thematic décor, we will work with you to create a stunning setup that fits your event’s vision.

4. How do I choose the right bed net for my space?

We offer a variety of bed nets in different sizes and styles. Our team will help you select a net that not only fits your bed but also complements your bedroom décor. All our nets are designed to provide both aesthetic appeal and effective protection from insects.

5. What training lessons do you offer, and who can join?

We offer courses in fashion design, curtain making, and event decoration. Our lessons are open to everyone, from beginners to those looking to refine their skills. The courses are hands-on, and we provide all the necessary materials to help you learn effectively.
